Game Overview

Explore a world where history and mythology intertwine in this open-world RPG set in ancient Egypt.

This game blends real historical events with mythical elements, offering players a chance to experience ancient Egypt in a unique and engaging way, with a focus on survival, strategy, and deep narrative choices.

Story and Setting

Experience a narrative-driven journey set in a rich, immersive world where your choices shape the fate of a dying dynasty.

Set in a world inspired by ancient Egypt, the story follows the crumbling of a once-great empire, with players influencing the outcome through their decisions and interactions with key characters and factions.

Legacy of Atum: Dead Dynasty

"Legacy of Atum: Dead Dynasty" is a story-driven, open-world action RPG for PC, set in ancient Egypt. Blending historical accuracy with mythological elements, the game challenges players with survival mechanics, complex combat, and strategic gameplay as they navigate the turmoil of a dying dynasty. Players will experience a deeply immersive world, uncover hidden lore, and make impactful choices that shape their journey.
